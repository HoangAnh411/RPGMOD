# Season Structure

## Season Length

90 ngày (3 tháng).

## Season Break

7-14 ngày giữa các mùa.

Dùng để deploy update, fix bug, chuẩn bị mùa mới.

---

# Season Theme

Mỗi mùa có 1 theme.

Ví dụ:

Season 1: Fire & Fury (focus Fire content)

Season 2: Frozen Throne (focus Ice content)

Season 3: Thunder Call (focus Lightning content)

Season 4: Abyssal Tide (focus Water content + Void)

---

# Season Pass

## Free Track

20 tiers.

Rewards: Gold, Materials, Basic Cosmetics, EXP Booster.

## Premium Track

50 tiers.

Rewards: Exclusive Cosmetics, Mount Skin, Weapon Skin, Titles, Auras.

Yêu cầu: Season Pass Ticket (Gold hoặc Premium).

## Tier Progression

Tăng tier qua:

Season Quest hoàn thành

Season EXP từ mọi hoạt động

Daily Login

---

# Season Quests

## Daily Season Quest

Reset hàng ngày.

3 quest ngẫu nhiên.

Reward: Season EXP + Gold.

Ví dụ:

Kill 50 monsters

Complete 1 Dungeon

Craft 5 items

## Weekly Season Quest

Reset hàng tuần.

5 quest.

Reward: Season EXP ×3 + Materials.

Ví dụ:

Clear 5 Dungeons

Defeat 3 Bosses

Complete 10 quests

## Seasonal Challenge

1 lần / mùa.

Khó, reward cao.

Ví dụ:

Reach Floor X

Kill Raid Boss

Achieve 1000 Achievement Points
