# Vision Ranks

## Rank 1 — Initiate

Yêu cầu: Vision Level 1

Bonus:

+5% Element Damage

Không có Passive

---

## Rank 2 — Adept

Yêu cầu: Vision Level 3

Bonus:

+8% Element Damage

Passive Slot: 1

Ví dụ Passive: Elemental Affinity (+5% Element Scaling)

---

## Rank 3 — Disciple

Yêu cầu: Vision Level 5

Bonus:

+12% Element Damage

Passive Slot: 2

---

## Rank 4 — Master

Yêu cầu: Vision Level 7

Bonus:

+16% Element Damage

Passive Slot: 2

Unlock: Elemental Aura (visual effect)

---

## Rank 5 — Grandmaster

Yêu cầu: Vision Level 10

Bonus:

+20% Element Damage

Passive Slot: 3

Unlock: Signature Effect

---

# Ascension

Sau Rank 5, Vision có thể Ascension.

## Ascension 1 — Awakened

Yêu cầu:

Vision Rank 5

Elemental Core × 1 (từ Element Boss)

Boss Soul × 5

Gold × 100000

Bonus:

+25% Element Damage

Passive Slot: 3

Unlock: Awakened Aura

Ascension Passive: True Element (+10% Element Penetration)

---

## Ascension 2 — Transcendent

Yêu cầu:

Awakened Vision

Primordial Essence × 1 (từ Raid Boss)

Mythic Essence × 1

Gold × 500000

Bonus:

+30% Element Damage

Passive Slot: 4

Unlock: Transcendent Aura

Ascension Passive: Elemental Reign (+15% Reaction Damage)

---

## Ascension 3 — Divine (Max)

Yêu cầu:

Transcendent Vision

Divine Core × 1 (world event exclusive)

Boss Soul × 20

Gold × 2000000

Bonus:

+40% Element Damage

Passive Slot: 5

Unlock: Divine Aura

Ascension Passive: God's Element (Double Reaction Damage)

# Signature Effects by Element

## Fire Vision — Inferno Core

Signature Effect: Immolate

Mỗi đòn Fire Damage gây thêm Burn DOT.

DOT Damage: INT × 0.3 mỗi giây

Duration: 3 giây

## Water Vision — Ocean Core

Signature Effect: Tidal Blessing

Mỗi lần nhận Heal, hồi thêm 10% Mana.

+20% Healing Received

## Ice Vision — Frost Core

Signature Effect: Absolute Zero

Freeze không bị break bởi damage trong 1 giây đầu.

+50% Freeze Duration

## Lightning Vision — Storm Core

Signature Effect: Overcharge

Mỗi Crit tăng 5% Attack Speed (stack 5 lần).

Stack Duration: 5 giây

# Vision Passives Pool

## Common Passives (available to all elements)

Elemental Affinity: +5% Element Scaling

Mana Efficiency: -10% Mana Cost

Rapid Cast: +5% Skill Speed

Elemental Shield: +5% Element Resistance

Overflow: 10% chance double element proc

## Fire Passives

Inferno Soul: +15% Burn Damage

Flame Burst: Kill enemy → small AOE explosion

Heat Wave: +5% Fire Penetration

## Water Passives

Pure Water: +10% Healing

Mana Spring: +10 Mana Regen

Cleanse: Remove 1 debuff mỗi 30 giây

## Ice Passives

Shatter: Break Freeze → bonus damage

Cold Front: Nearby enemies slowed 10%

Ice Barrier: 5% Max HP shield mỗi 30 giây

## Lightning Passives

Static Field: Nearby enemies take shock damage

Quick Step: Dodge cost reduced 5 Stamina

Chain Reaction: 10% chain to extra target

# Vision Materials

## Elemental Core

Nhận từ: Element Boss (guaranteed drop)

Element Boss spawn: Weekly

## Primordial Essence

Nhận từ: Raid Boss (low chance)

## Divine Core

Nhận từ: World Event (rare spawn)

## Vision Shard

Nhận từ: Dungeon Boss (common)

Dùng để: Vision Level Up (Rank 1-5)

---

# Vision Reset

Có thể reset Vision passives.

Yêu cầu: Vision Reset Crystal + Gold × 10000

Reset về Rank 1, hoàn trả 50% material.
